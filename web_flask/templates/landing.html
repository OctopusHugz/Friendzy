About Us

The original idea for Friendzy began as a simple note on a phone a handful of years ago. The inspiration was the realization of the toxicity of social media. Young people in today's society are experiencing mental health issues at levels never seen before, with a lot of the damage attributable to social media. We at Friendzy could see people were more disconnected than ever, and decided we wanted to step in and change things.
